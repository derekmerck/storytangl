stats
=====

- provide a mechanism for introducing some randomness and strategy into a story.
- represent a scale of competence within a domain, i.e., body, mind
- can be tested against tasks of various difficulty 
- can be improved through training or tests
- underlying continuous value represented by discrete quantiles for narrative purposes, for example, very easy, easy, average, hard, very hard.  5 quantiles seems about right, but it's easy enough to add more.
